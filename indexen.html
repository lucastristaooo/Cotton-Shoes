About us

The story of those who bring you comfort

In 2000, Cotton Shoes was founded in Franca, Sao Paulo, with the mission to create a high-quality, innovative, sustainable, timely, and diverse footwear brand. From the beginning, the company specialized in producing custom-made shoes for renowned brands, investing in cutting-edge technology and a highly skilled team. Cotton Shoes adopts sustainable practices throughout the production chain, seeking to minimize environmental impacts and offer an inclusive and diverse work environment. With these values and practices, Cotton Shoes has gained the trust and partnership of several renowned brands, becoming a reference in Private Label and exporting to various countries around the world.
